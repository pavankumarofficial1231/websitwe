Video Library FAQ

How do I access the video library?

Our video library is available to all registered schools and students. Simply visit the Video Library section and log in with your credentials provided by your school administrator.

Are the videos available for download?

For copyright and licensing reasons, most videos are only available for streaming. However, some workshop materials and presentations may be available for download.

What topics are covered in the video library?

Our library includes tutorials on AI & Robotics, Web Development, Financial Literacy, plus recordings of past workshops and guest lectures from industry experts.

Can I request specific video content?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from students and educators. Use the Contact form to send us your requests.
